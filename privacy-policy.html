Scholar AI Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2025

1. Introduction

Scholar AI (“we”, “us”, or “our”) provides a mobile application that helps users solve math and science problems using AI and OCR technology. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you register, we collect your email, password, display name, and (optionally) profile photo.
  • Usage Data: We track your question history, favorites, and usage statistics to provide app features.
  • Uploaded Content: Images and text you upload for processing are temporarily stored and sent to third-party APIs (OpenAI, Mathpix) for analysis.
  • Purchase Data: Subscription and purchase status are managed via RevenueCat.
  • Automatically Collected Data: Firebase may collect device and usage data for security and performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve app features
  • To authenticate users and manage accounts
  • To process your questions and images using third-party AI services
  • To manage subscriptions and purchases
  • To communicate with you about your account or app updates

4. How We Share Your Information

Third-Party Processors: We share data with:

  • OpenAI: For AI-powered answers (your questions/text)
  • Mathpix: For math OCR (your images)
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management (purchase data)
  • Firebase: For authentication, storage, and database

We do not sell your data or share it for advertising purposes.

5. Data Retention

  • Your account and history are retained until you delete your account.
  • Uploaded images are stored temporarily for processing and then deleted.
  • You may delete your account at any time, which will erase your data from our systems.

6. Your Rights

  • Access & Correction: You can view and edit your profile information.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data.
  • Consent: By using the app, you consent to this policy and the processing of your data as described.

7. Security

  • We use industry-standard security measures, including Firebase Authentication and secure storage.
  • API keys and sensitive data are protected.

8. Children’s Privacy

The app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

Notification Clause: We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or by other means, and, where required by law, obtain your consent.

10. Contact Us

For questions or concerns, contact: support@scholar-ai.io
